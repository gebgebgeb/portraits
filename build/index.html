If you would like to be the artist:

  1. Send the "sitter link" url to your friend
  2. Wait until they have opened the page
  3. Load the "drawer link" in your browser
  4. Begin drawing!

If you would like to be drawn:

  1. Open the "sitter link" in your browser
  2. Send the "drawer link" url to your friend
  3. Wait for your friend
  4. Be drawn!
